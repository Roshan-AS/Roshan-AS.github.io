## PLEASE READ THROUGH BEFORE E-MAILING ME

I am looking for strongly motivated postdoctoral researchers, PhD, and MS students interested in building deployable, real-world systems that can make a difference using wireless and mobile systems. I am also seeking students with strong background in machine learning, signal processing, wireless systems and who have strong coding skills who are eager to make Next generation of wireless and mobile systems more efficient and more reliable. Feel free to drop me an email with your CV with the following in mind...


### Applications for PhD and Postdoc positions
If you are interested in working with me as a PhD student or postdoctoral scholar, please send an email directly to **roshana [at] buffalo.edu**. State briefly why you are interested and attach a CV (possibly a link to your webpage). No need for a separate cover letter or certificates. **Important**: please insert _"Application PhD"_ or _"Application Postdoc"_ in the subject line. 

### UB Students
If you are a student at UB CSE or UB in general looking for a project, contact me through email or stop by my office (Davis 328). 

### Bachelor's / Master's students from elsewhere
If you are interested in pursuing a Master's degree at UB, see [https://engineering.buffalo.edu/computer-science-engineering/graduate/admissions.html](https://engineering.buffalo.edu/computer-science-engineering/graduate/admissions.html).

### Expected Skills
Experience in at least two or more of the following would greatly help: Wireless Networks, Sensor Systems, Machine Learning, Convex optimization, Signal Processing, and SLAM automation
